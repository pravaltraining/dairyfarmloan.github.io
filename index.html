Financial Assistance For Dairy Farming

DCB Dairy Loan is a specially designed unsecured term loan tailored for farmers to support various aspects of their dairy farming activities. Whether you want to purchase cattle, construct cattle sheds, develop infrastructure, or acquire feedstock; this loan provides financial assistance to meet your diverse needs. DCB Bank offers Dairy loans through Business Correspondent (BC) channel .

Highlights

Type of Loan

Term Loan.

Tenure

6 to 36 months.

Loan Purpose

Purchase of cattle, construction of cattle shed, infrastructure development, purchase of feedstock.

Loan Amount

₹ 50,000 - ₹ 3 Lakh.

Repayment Frequency

Monthly.

Fee

No penalty and prepayment charges.

Eligibility

Individuals under the Small and Marginal Farmer category.

Dairy Farmers.

Frequently Asked Questions

To apply for the loan, you’ll need to submit an application form along with KYC (Know Your Customer) documents.

It takes approx. 15 working days to process the loan application

Defaulting on loan repayment can lead to penalties, legal action, and adverse impact to your credit score.
